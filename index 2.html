The Call

Shallow deficit, ~2,150 kcal. You're a detrained lifter — you regain muscle fast for the next 2–3 months no matter what. A shallow deficit lets fat drop and that rebound happen at once. That's the recomp.

Don't suffer. Win on consistency and protein, not starvation. Target ~0.25kg/week down. Checkpoint at ~78–79kg, then reassess for a lean bulk.
