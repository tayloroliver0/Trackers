Koda
Fast learner · Selective compliance · Tests boundaries
Phase 1
Immediate — Weeks 1–4
Nothing In Life Is Free
Critical
Start now, maintain forever
Every reward (food, pets, freedom, attention) requires a command first. Sit before meals, before going out, before getting pets. Fixes selective compliance at the root.
  • Never give food, freedom, or affection without a command first
  • If he refuses — wait. Don't repeat. Withhold reward until he complies
  • Even 5 seconds of sit before his bowl goes down counts
  • Every person on the property must do this consistently
Recall on Long Line
Critical
4–8 weeks to reliable
Attach a 5–10m rope to his collar. Call once. If he ignores, gentle steady pressure — no yanking. Reward heavily on arrival. Never punish after recall.
  • Call only ONCE — repeating teaches him ignoring is an option
  • High value treat for recall only (meat/cheese) — nothing else gets this reward
  • Practice in low distraction first, add distractions gradually
  • Never chase him — use the line calmly
  • Always reward arrival even if it took forever
Bite & Jump Inhibition
High
1–2 weeks with full consistency
Sharp "OW" + immediate complete withdrawal of attention. Turn back, cross arms, zero eye contact. Walk away if he persists. No physical corrections — any reaction is still engagement.
  • Any tooth contact = you disappear immediately
  • Jump AND bite = leave the area entirely
  • Every person on property must react identically
  • Exercise him before energy peaks in the evening
Home / Pen Command
High
2–4 weeks
Use the long line. Practice sending him to the pen with a high value reward waiting inside. Do this randomly during the day — not just at night. Pen = good things.
  • Practice 3–5x daily at random times, not just at bedtime
  • High value treat waiting inside pen every time
  • Say "home" once calmly, guide with line if needed, reward on arrival
  • Never make going to the pen feel like punishment
Phase 2
Building — Weeks 4–8
Recall Off Leash
Medium
Only after long line recall is 100% solid
Move off the long line only when he's coming every single time on it. Start in low distraction, test with distractions. Go back to long line if reliability drops.
  • A solid long line recall beats a shaky off-leash one — don't rush
  • Test off leash in the pen area first (contained)
  • Retreat to long line immediately if he ignores once
Stay
Medium
After recall is reliable
Sit or down, say "stay," take one step back, return and reward. Build duration first, then distance, then distractions. Return to him to reward — don't call him out of stay initially.
  • Order: duration first (30 sec) → then distance → then distractions
  • Always return to him to reward, not call him out of stay
  • If he breaks, reset without punishment and shorten duration

  • Don't set a date — let his behavior determine the timeline
  • Gate exit MUST be physically closed before this is safe
  • Rocky will likely earn this back before Koda — that's fine
